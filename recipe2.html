Brownie

Let's get started!

Ingredients
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
Instructions
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter. Alternatively, you can melt it on the stove in a saucepan over low heat.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and sugar. Stir until well combined.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Avoid overmixing to prevent a dense texture.
  7. If desired, stir in the chopped nuts and chocolate chips.
  8. Pour the brownie batter into the greased baking dish, spreading it evenly.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Be careful not to overbake.
  10. Remove the brownies from the oven and let them cool completely in the baking dish before cutting into squares.
Enjoy your homemade Brownies!
