Tiramisu

Let's get started!

Ingredients
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/2 cups brewed espresso or strong coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 8 ounces mascarpone cheese
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
Instructions
  1. In a mixing bowl, beat the egg yolks and sugar together until thick and pale yellow.
  2. In a saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it begins to simmer. Remove from heat.
  3. Slowly pour the hot milk into the egg yolk mixture, whisking constantly.
  4. Transfer the mixture back into the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  5. In a separate mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  6. In another bowl, whisk the mascarpone cheese until smooth. Add in the cooled custard mixture and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  7. Gently fold in the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until fully incorporated.
  8. Dip each ladyfinger into the brewed espresso or coffee, just enough to moisten them without soaking completely. Place them in a single layer in the bottom of a rectangular or square dish.
  9. Spread half of the mascarpone cream mixture evenly over the ladyfingers.
  10. Dust a layer of cocoa powder over the cream.
  11. Repeat the process with another layer of dipped ladyfingers, followed by the remaining cream mixture, and a final dusting of cocoa powder on top.
  12. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to allow the flavors to meld and the tiramisu to set.
  13. When ready to serve, cut into portions and dust with additional cocoa powder if desired.
Enjoy your homemade Tiramisu!
